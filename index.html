Welcome to Foghorn Unscripted

We are an established group of comedy improvisers from Birmingham, UK. We perform improvised stories, using nothing but suggestions from the audience and the power of our own imaginations.

The Old Joint Stock

18th July 2024

An Improvised Murder

Birmingham Fest

An evening of tales, laughter, games… and an impending demise!

The script is unwritten and the plot is in your hands. Step into a world of suspense, laughter, and betrayal as you help us solve the mystery of whodunit using your own sleuthing skills.

No two shows are ever the same.

Winterbourne House and Garden

3rd & 4th August 2024 - 6pm

Murder on the Lawn

Back for another year of murder and mayhem!

Foghorn Unscripted return to entertain you with their comedic homage to those quintessentially British Whodunits… Was it the Butler or the impoverished Lady of the Manor? Did they use a candle stick or a feather duster to carry out their murderous antics. Just how was the telephone involved?!

We need you, the audience, to join our experienced performers and help unmask the murderer.

This hilarious show comes to the historic Winterbourne House and Garden for two special outdoor performances in August We promise: no audience members will be harmed in the making of these shows!
